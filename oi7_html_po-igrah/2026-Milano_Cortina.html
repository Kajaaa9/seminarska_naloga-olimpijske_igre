        FAQ

        Here are the answers to your questions.

        • When are the Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games Milano Cortina 2026 taking place?

          The Olympic Winter Games Milano Cortina 2026 will be held from 6 to 22 February 2026 and the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Winter Games from 6 to 15 March 2026.

        • Where will the Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games Milano Cortina 2026 be held?

          The Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games Milano Cortina 2026 will be spread over an area of more than 22,000 sq km. In addition to Milano and Cortina d'Ampezzo, competitions and ceremonies will also take place in Bormio and Livigno in Valtellina, Predazzo and Tesero in Val di Fiemme, Anterselva/Antholz and Verona. Discover all the venues on the interactive map.

        • How can I join Team26, the Volunteers Programme for Milano Cortina 2026? 

          Applications to join Team26 are officially open. Visit team26.milanocortina2026.org to get all the details and submit your application online.

        • How and when can I purchase tickets?

          Tickets are exclusively digital and available only through the official ticketing platform (tickets.milanocortina2026.org). Each person can purchase up to 25 tickets. You can find all the information about how to buy Olympic and Paralympic tickets, official hospitality opportunities and travel and accommodation packages by clicking on the Ticketing and Hospitality sections in the side menu of our website, or on the dedicated buttons on the Homepage.

        • When and where can I buy official Milano Cortina 2026 merchandise?

          You can buy products from the first Milano Cortina 2026 clothing collections and official merchandise on our online shop. Additional references will be put on sale in the coming months. Check out all available products by clicking on the Shop button at the top of the page.
